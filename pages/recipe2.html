Pancakes

Ohh, that savoury and delicious dose of saccharin sweetness!!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Cooking Time

20 mins (Approx)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot griddle and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook until golden brown.

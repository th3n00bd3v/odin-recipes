Pancakes

Ohh, that savoury and delicious dose of saccharin sweetness!!

Ingredients

  • Batter
  • Vanilla Powder
  • Apple Puree
  • Baking Soda
  • Cake Frosting (optional)

Cooking Time: 45-50 mins (Approx)

Instructions for cooking

  1. Mix the batter, vanilla powder, apple puree and baking soda thoroughly.
  2. Bake it in the oven for atleast 5 mins.
  3. Once it is baked, layer it with cream frosting and refrigerate it for 1-2 hours.

A great replacement for birthday cakes, don't you think?

Lasagna

Yes, Garfield's favorite food is mine too!

Ingredients

  • Cheese (like, a whole lot of it!!)
  • Vegetables (except Broccoli)
  • Tomato Puree

Cooking Time

30 mins (Approx)

Instructions

  1. Boil the vegetables in a medium flame and add a little water.
  2. Once they are boiled, add the cheese and saute thoroughly.
  3. Add the tomato puree for the extra tangi-ness!

Once it simmers down, you can gulp the whole thing in one go!

Enjoy!

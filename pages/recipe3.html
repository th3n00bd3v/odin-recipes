Thin Crust Pizza

They don't make it thinner than this anywhere in the world!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Your favorite pizza toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Cooking Time

15 mins (Approx)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to a thin crust.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
